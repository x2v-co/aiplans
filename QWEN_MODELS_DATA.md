## Notes

1. **MoE Models**: Models labeled with "A" (active) are Mixture-of-Experts models where only a subset of parameters are active during inference. For example, `qwen3-235b-a22b-instruct` has 235B total parameters but only 22B are active.

2. **Thinking Models**: Models with "thinking" suffix are designed for complex reasoning tasks and may use a chain-of-thought approach. They typically have higher pricing due to increased computational requirements.

3. **Context Windows**: Standard context is typically 32K tokens. Long context variants support 128K or even 1M tokens for document processing applications.

4. **Pricing**: Prices are per 1M tokens. Input and output prices are the same for most models, but may differ for some specialized models.

5. **Currency**: All prices are in CNY (¥) as Qwen is an Alibaba Cloud product.

6. **API Endpoint**: The base URL for all Qwen models is `https://dashscope.aliyuncs.com/api/v1/services/aigc/text-generation/generation`
