## Notes

1. **Currency**: All prices are in CNY (¥)
2. **Token Units**: Prices shown as "元/千tokens" means CNY per 1,000 tokens
3. **Volume Discounts**: Many models offer tiered pricing based on token volume
4. **Cached Tokens**: Some models offer significant discounts (up to 75-80%) for cached input tokens
5. **Promotional Period**: ERNIE-4.5-Turbo-32K and DeepSeek series have limited-time discounts (Feb 2 - Mar 31, 2026)
6. **TPM/RPM Limits**: Some models have specific TPM (tokens per minute) and RPM (requests per minute) limits
7. **Thinking Mode**: Models with "Thinking" suffix may have different pricing when thinking mode is enabled
8. **Context Windows**: Vary by model, typically 8K, 32K, 128K, or 200K tokens
